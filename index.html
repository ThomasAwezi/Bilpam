About Us

Bilpam Telecommunications Co. Ltd is a Juba-based company providing satellite communications and information technology services across South Sudan. Founded in 2006, Bilpam serves corporations, government institutions, NGOs, and residential customers with reliable VSAT, broadband, fiber and networking solutions.

Head Office: Bilpam Road, Juba, South Sudan
