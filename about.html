About Bilpam Telecommunications

Bilpam Telecommunications Co. Ltd was founded in 2006 and has since been a leading provider of satellite communications and ICT services in South Sudan. We deliver VSAT, broadband, fiber and managed networking services to NGOs, government agencies, banks, educational institutions, and residential users.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, secure and accessible communication services that support development and economic activity across South Sudan.

Our Vision

To be South Sudan's trusted partner for connectivity and ICT solutions.
